  • Alakh Pandey, a physics instructor, began his YouTube channel “PhysicsWallah” on January 27, 2014. He used to teach in coaching institutes before that.
  • He began teaching physics and chemistry to pupils in the 10th, 11th, and 12th grades. He engages in lighthearted banter with his students and even breaks out into song while instructing.
  • He sings Bollywood songs at random and adapts the words to the situation. For example, he once sang, “Aankhon mein base ho tum, tumhe dil mein basa lunga.
